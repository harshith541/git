About the Festival

Maha Shivaratri is one of the most important Hindu festivals dedicated to Lord Shiva. Devotees observe fasting, night vigil, and offer prayers to seek blessings.

Significance

It is believed that worshipping Lord Shiva on this day brings peace, prosperity, and removes negativity from life.
